# Connecting Discord and Patreon
Connecting your Discord account to Patreon allows you to access Discord servers that are linked to your Patreon subscriptions and receive roles based on your membership tier.

---

## Accessing Patreon's "Connected Apps"
Navigate to [Patreon](https://patreon.com) and click the "Settings" button on the left side of the page.

<img src="https://assets.martysmods.com/additionalguides/patreon/PatreonSettingsButton.webp" alt="PatreonSettingsButton" style={{maxWidth: '600px'}} />

In the settings menu, click the "More..." dropdown menu.

<img src="https://assets.martysmods.com/additionalguides/patreon/PatreonSettingsMoreDropdown.webp" alt="PatreonSettingsMoreDropdown" style={{maxWidth: '600px'}} />

Select "Connected Apps" from the dropdown menu.

<img src="https://assets.martysmods.com/additionalguides/patreon/PatreonSettingsConnectedApps.webp" alt="PatreonSettingsConnectedApps" style={{maxWidth: '600px'}} />

This will be the place in Patreon where you can bind all sorts of different application's accounts to your Patreon account for roles and perks that Patreon integrates with.

---

## Connecting Your Discord Account
Click on the "Discord" integration in the Connected Apps panel.

<img src="https://assets.martysmods.com/additionalguides/patreon/ConnectPatreonDiscordConnectedApp.webp" alt="PatreonConnectedAppDiscord" style={{maxWidth: '600px'}} />

Click the "Connect" button to link your Discord account to Patreon.

<img src="https://assets.martysmods.com/additionalguides/patreon/ConnectPatreonDiscordConnectButton.webp" alt="PatreonConnectedAppDiscordConnectButton" style={{maxWidth: '600px'}} />

If you're not already logged into Discord, you'll be prompted to sign in.

<img src="https://assets.martysmods.com/additionalguides/patreon/ConnectPatreonDiscordLogin.webp" alt="PatreonDiscordSignIn" style={{maxWidth: '600px'}} />

Authorize Patreon to access your Discord account by clicking "Authorize".

<img src="https://assets.martysmods.com/additionalguides/patreon/ConnectPatreonDiscordAuthorize.webp" alt="PatreonDiscordAuthorize" style={{maxWidth: '600px'}} />

After authorizing, your Discord account name will appear in the Connected Apps panel, and you'll be able to join Discord servers that are linked to your current Patreon subscriptions.
